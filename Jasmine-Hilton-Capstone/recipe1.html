Frozen Blueberry Coconut Oil Dog Treats

This recipe was written by: Jessica H

Required Items

  • Small silicone heart mold
  • ½ cup coconut oil
  • Frozen blueberries

Instructions

  1. Place a frozen blueberry into each heart in the silicone mold. Set aside
  2. In a small saucepan, heat coconut oil until it liquefies.
  3. Allow coconut oil to cool for a few minutes, then carefully pour oil into the heart mold.
  4. Carefully place mold into the freezer to solidify.
  5. When coconut oil treats are solid, pop them out of the mold.
  6. Store coconut oil treats in the freezer.
